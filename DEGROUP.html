Discipleship & Evangelism (DE) Group: Purpose and Goals

Purpose

The DE Group exists to cultivate a lifestyle of faithful discipleship and bold evangelism. Rooted in the Great Commission (Matthew 28:19–20), this group is a space where believers are encouraged, equipped, and sent out to make disciples who make disciples.

Core Rhythms of the Group

D - Discipleship

  • Growing in Christlikeness through Scripture, prayer, and accountability. (Romans 12:2, 2 Peter 3:18)
  • Practicing spiritual disciplines. (1 Timothy 4:7–8)

E - Evangelism

  • Sharing the gospel in daily life. (Romans 1:16, Acts 1:8)
  • Intentional outreach. (Colossians 4:5–6)
  • Testimonies and prayer. (Philemon 1:6, Hebrews 10:24–25)

FACT Qualities

  • Faithful: Reliable in their relationship with God. (Luke 16:10, Proverbs 20:6)
  • Available: Willing to prioritize God. (Isaiah 6:8, Matthew 4:19–20)
  • Teachable: Humble and ready to grow. (Proverbs 9:9, 2 Timothy 3:16–17)
  • Committed: Devoted to Christ and the mission. (Luke 9:23, John 15:5, Romans 12:11)

Weekly Structure

Highs and Lows

Life updates and prayer. (Galatians 6:2, Romans 12:15, James 5:16, Psalm 34:17)

Ins and Outs

Scripture memory and evangelism reports. (Psalm 119:11, Joshua 1:8, Mark 16:15, 1 Peter 3:15)

HEAR Journal

  • Highlight: A verse that stands out. (Psalm 119:105)
  • Explain: Describe the meaning. (2 Timothy 2:15)
  • Apply: How it impacts you. (James 1:22–25)
  • Respond: A prayer or action. (Romans 12:1, Psalm 19:14)

Long-Term Goal

Encouraging members to lead their own DE Groups. (Matthew 9:37–38, 2 Timothy 2:2)

Our Aim

  • Grow in Christ (Colossians 2:6–7)
  • Share faith confidently (Ephesians 6:19–20)
  • Make disciples (Matthew 28:19–20)
  • Live with gospel joy (Philippians 1:27, John 15:11)
"What you have heard from me in the presence of many witnesses, commit to faithful men who will be able to teach others also." — 2 Timothy 2:2

Sample Member Commitments

  • Grow in Christ
  • Attend and participate
  • Engage with Scripture
  • Share the gospel
  • Encourage others
  • Be open to leading a group

FAQ

What if I’ve never shared the gospel before?

That’s okay! You’ll be supported and equipped. (Matthew 10:19–20)

Do I have to lead a DE Group after this?

We encourage it, but if not ready, you may rejoin or join another group. (2 Timothy 2:2)

What if I miss a session?

Let someone know and catch up—grace over perfection.

Can I invite others?

Yes, but group size is capped at 5–7. Commitment is required.

How long does the group last?

We meet every other week. Duration depends on the book studied.
